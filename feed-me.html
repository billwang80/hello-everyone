devpost
A project created to help hungry users find recipes or restuarants depending on available ingredients, cuisine style preference, location, and budget. Catered to foodies or cooks looking for new tastes by removing the hassle of toiling through recipe books and restuarant guides.
The project was inspired by my notorious indecisiveness when it comes to food. It was built to help make fast, informed decisions within the desired constraints. Features I would like to add:
  • user recipes + reviews
  • ordering dishes or ingredients
  • nutrition info
