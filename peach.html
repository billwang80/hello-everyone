App designed for people to practice their public speaking skills. Record the audio for your speech or presentation and receive feedback about your displayed emotion, tone, and language style. Hone your speech skills with Peach to achieve your desired speaking goal.
I created this project for a company hackathon during my time at Trend Micro using React, Express, Speech Recognition API, and IBM Tone API. I noticed that many people have problems with public speaking, especially with large audiences or under pressure so I created Peach to help with nerves by encouraging practice. Some features I would like to add:
  • audio analysis (waveform)
  • user progression
  • facial expression recognition
